Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Revit CADmep Guide website, including the Caddy chatbot feature.

1. Information We Collect

This website is designed to minimise data collection. We do not require user accounts or personal details to use the site.

We may handle the following information:

  • Messages entered into the Caddy chatbot
  • Basic technical data such as browser type and device (via standard web access)

2. Caddy Chatbot Data

The Caddy chatbot stores conversations locally in your browser using local storage.

Important:
  • Chat data is stored only on your device
  • We do not transmit or store your chat messages on any server
  • Clearing your browser data will remove all saved conversations

3. How We Use Information

Any information entered into the site is used only to:

  • Provide responses within the chatbot
  • Improve your on-site experience
  • Maintain basic website functionality

4. Cookies and Local Storage

This site does not use tracking cookies for advertising. However, it uses local storage for:

  • Saving chatbot conversations
  • Improving usability

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal data to third parties.

6. Data Security

As no personal data is stored on external servers, your information remains under your control within your browser.

7. Your Rights (UK GDPR)

Under UK GDPR, you have rights over your data, including:

  • The right to access your data
  • The right to delete your data
  • The right to restrict processing

Since all chatbot data is stored locally, you can exercise these rights by clearing your browser storage.

8. Third-Party Services

This website may link to external sites such as Autodesk. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be reflected on this page.
